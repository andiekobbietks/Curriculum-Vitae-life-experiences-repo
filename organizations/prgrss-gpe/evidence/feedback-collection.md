## Metadata

## Description

Established and managed a comprehensive user feedback collection process for the PRGRSS mentorship app. Combined structured surveys with individual mentee conversations to gather actionable insights that shaped product development priorities.

---

## Context

### Situation
PRGRSS needed systematic feedback from users to improve the app experience. Previous feedback was sporadic and unstructured, making it difficult to prioritize development efforts.

### Task/Challenge
- Create structured feedback collection mechanism
- Gather honest user feedback on app experience
- Identify patterns and priorities from diverse user input
- Communicate insights to development team and leadership

### Action
1. Created Google Forms feedback survey
2. Actively promoted survey in the Career App WhatsApp group
3. Conducted individual phone calls with mentees
4. Synthesized feedback into actionable tickets and requirements
5. Reported insights to leadership in concise formats

### Result
- Collected multiple rounds of structured feedback
- Identified critical bugs and UX issues
- Gathered feature requests that shaped roadmap (job listings)
- Built trust with users through personal engagement

## Key Feedback Themes

### 1. Call Quality Issues

Users reported technical problems with the calling feature:
- Sound issues during calls
- Network dropouts
- Call failures requiring manual intervention

> "When I had my call, there was a sound issue so I didn't get to speak with the mentor" - Priscilla

### 2. Booking & Credits System

Confusion around call credits and booking limitations:
- Users unclear on how credits worked
- No-show penalties affected user experience
- Need for admin panel to manage exceptions

### 3. Feature Requests: Jobs & Opportunities

Strong demand for career opportunity features:

> "Would you like us to put job listings on the app or internships" - AndrÃ©
> Response was overwhelmingly positive with detailed suggestions:

- Apprenticeships (degree and masters/level 3-5)
- Internships
- Graduate programmes
- Social mobility internships
- Mentor-to-job matching

### 4. Discovery & Matching

Users wanted better ways to find relevant mentors:

> "Let me just share it here real quick... Or if it's not tinder-like it could be a mini questionnaire or something in the search to filter out certain things... And then it recommends the top 10 people based on your filtering" - Eden Dion

### 5. Calendar Integration

Users needed help managing their bookings:

> "Does anyone know how to add the meeting info from the app to our Google calendar please?" - Eden Dion

## Feedback Synthesis Process

### Step 1: Collection
Gathered feedback through multiple channels simultaneously

### Step 2: Analysis
Identified patterns and categorized issues:
- Bugs (call failures, UI issues)
- UX problems (confusing flows, unclear limitations)
- Feature requests (job listings, better matching)

### Step 3: Documentation
Created actionable tickets for development team:
> "I know my role has been figuring out the HOW of things so will be doubling down day to day and get more traction with individualised calls and reach out"

### Step 4: Reporting
Provided updates to leadership in their preferred format:
> "Just prefer high level it's done type of messages... E.g here is a folder with 10 testimonials written and video for someone to use on socials"

## Key Learnings

1. **Personal touch matters**: Individual calls revealed insights surveys missed
2. **Users are generous**: When asked properly, users provide detailed, thoughtful feedback
3. **Synthesis takes time**: Quality analysis requires dedicated effort
4. **Format matters**: Present feedback in format leadership prefers (concise, actionable)
