## Description

Delivered STEM workshops in schools across Wales as a Technocamps Student Ambassador. Sessions focused on inspiring young people about computer science, coding, and technology careers using hands-on activities with LEGO Mindstorms, Micro:bits, and other educational technology.

---

## Context

### Situation
Technocamps runs an ongoing programme of school workshops to inspire young people about STEM careers and computer science education. The programme relies on trained student ambassadors to deliver engaging sessions.

### Task/Challenge
Deliver high-quality, engaging workshops that introduce young people to coding and computational thinking concepts while managing logistics like equipment setup and coordination with school staff.

### Action
- Attended workshop training and preparation sessions
- Coordinated travel arrangements with team members
- Delivered hands-on coding sessions in various school settings
- Participated in special sessions including Ukrainian refugee children workshop
- Assisted with STEM careers talks

### Result
Successfully delivered multiple workshops across Wales, contributing to Technocamps' mission of inspiring the next generation of computer scientists and technologists.
