## Key Insights ðŸ’¡

### Technical Insights

1. **Agentic AI Architecture**: The value in AI agents is in the "wrapper" - the domain expertise and integration quality, not just the underlying models
2. **Accessibility as Foundation**: Hyper-legibility and cognitive load management aren't nice-to-haves but essential for quality UX
3. **Tool Ecosystem**: Modern development requires understanding the broader tool ecosystem (Firecrawl, Laminar, MCP) not just core frameworks

### Process Insights

1. **Sprint Planning Works**: Regular sprint planning and daily standups keep distributed teams aligned
2. **Documentation Prevents Issues**: Taking time to document decisions, architecture, and legal frameworks (NDAs) prevents future problems
3. **Collab Rooms**: Open collaborative sessions (Google Meet rooms) enable async co-working

### People/Communication Insights

1. **Transparency Builds Trust**: Being honest about availability and challenges strengthens relationships
2. **Shared Resources Amplify Learning**: Curating and sharing relevant resources accelerates team learning
3. **Lived Experience Adds Value**: Personal experience (as first-gen student, etc.) provides authentic insights that pure research cannot

## Reflection

This period of work across multiple organizations demonstrates my ability to contribute meaningfully to diverse teams and contexts. The common thread is a research-driven approach combined with strong communication and a commitment to accessibility and quality.

The main learning is the importance of realistic capacity assessment. Enthusiasm for meaningful work sometimes led to overcommitment, which required careful communication to manage. The solution is not to do less, but to be more intentional about where effort is invested and to communicate proactively about constraints.

The combination of technical skills (web development, UI/UX design) with strategic thinking (research, product strategy, competitive analysis) and soft skills (communication, knowledge sharing, accessibility advocacy) creates a unique value proposition that I should continue to develop and leverage.
