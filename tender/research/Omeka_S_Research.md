## Why Omeka S for UnMuseum

Omeka S is purpose-built for cultural heritage institutions. It has been trusted by museums, libraries, and archives worldwide for 10 years.

### Key Features for BSWN

| Feature | Benefit for UnMuseum |
|---------|---------------------|
| **Dublin Core Metadata** | International standard for heritage cataloguing |
| **Multi-site Architecture** | One installation, multiple themed collections |
| **Linked Open Data** | Connect to global heritage networks |
| **Media Management** | Images, audio, video, documents |
| **User Contributions** | Community members can submit stories |
| **Multilingual** | Content in community languages |
| **Open Source** | No licensing fees, full ownership |
| **API Access** | Integrate with AI services |
