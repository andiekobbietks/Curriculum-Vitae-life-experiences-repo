## Applications for UnMuseum

### 1. Oral History Voice Cloning

Preserve the voices of community elders before they pass:

- Record high-quality voice samples (with consent)
- Create voice clones for archival purposes
- Future generations hear stories in original voices
- Permanent heritage record

### 2. Accessibility

Text-to-speech for blind and low-vision users:

- All archive content readable aloud
- Voices in community dialects
- WCAG compliance

### 3. Interactive Guides

AI chatbot speaks in community voices:

- Guided archive exploration
- Engaging heritage experience
- Multiple language support
